Isaiah Dodo-Williams

Recent Graduate of Columbia's Master of Fine Arts Program

Isaiah Oluwadamilare Dodo-Williams Is an Nigerian/American actor. He was born in Lagos, Nigeria. When Isaiah was 6 years old, his parents were incredibly fortunate to be selected for the The Diversity Visa Lottery, allowing his parents and three older siblings to immigrate to the United States with full Citizenship.
Isaiah got his B.A from California State University San Bernardino and his M.F.A from Columbia University. IMDb
